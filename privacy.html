How your information is handled

This page explains what information is collected when you reach out, how it is stored, and the steps I take to keep your submissions secure and respectful.

What I collect

  • Contact form: Name, email, and the message you provide so I can respond.
  • Google intake form: Optional project context (team size, goals, timelines) that helps me prepare before a call.
  • Email correspondence: Any additional details you share when we continue the conversation.

How submissions are processed

Web form submissions are routed through Formspree using an encrypted HTTPS connection. The Google intake form is stored inside my Google Workspace account with access restricted to me. I download important notes into a private, access-controlled Notion workspace for follow-up actions.

I do not sell or rent your details. Information is only used to respond to your inquiry or to prepare for collaboration.

Data retention

Messages are reviewed quarterly. Items that are no longer relevant are deleted from Formspree, the Google intake spreadsheet, and my local notes. If we begin a project together, essential records are retained for operational or legal purposes.
